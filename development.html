Developing and maintaining AddGears

We aim to develop affordable platform useful to a broad user community. It is free for personal use and testing. It is not bound to any cloud provider and could be deployed on premises. If you find our services\applications useful, please sponsor AddGears development through donations. There are multiple options available. We are always happy to help our users with AddGears application or their software in general. Please contact us if you have any questions or ideas how to make AddGears better. You may think of AddGears as a community owned project.

Please note that the above implies that any code, documentation or ideas produced when jointly working on\discussing your business requirements later could be used to improve generally available AddGears application. AddGears is not affiliated with any of its business clients and doesn’t provide “private” software development. Our goal is to make AddGears affordable and open to everyone. If you want to keep some code, data or ideas confidential, you should take full responsibility for your business security and approach your problems accordingly: by analyzing your needs and security requirements, generating test data which won’t contain any sensitive information, hiring trusted specialist, etc.
